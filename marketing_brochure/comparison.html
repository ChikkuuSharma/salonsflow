Why Choose SalonsFlow?

A specialized product value framework tailored for regional beauty & grooming networks.

01

Maximize Margin & 0% Bookings Deduction

Major platforms like Fresha extract 20% of invoice revenues for bookings routed through their marketplaces. SalonsFlow operates as a pure SaaS, charging a simple flat subscription fee. You keep 100% of your customer billing returns.

02

Frictionless Hinglish Dialogue NLP Processing

Unlike international products restricted to standard English inputs, SalonsFlow processes mixed regional Hinglish voice note clips and text phrases natively. Customers book instantly on WhatsApp using their natural speaking style.

03

Immediate Missed Call Revenue Capture

Up to 30% of incoming salon booking calls are missed during busy weekend rushes. SalonsFlow's native call webhook captures missed rings and automatically sends a WhatsApp greeting with the scheduling menu, ensuring no customer walks away.

04

Google SEO post-visit WhatsApp rating Cards

Boost your local search maps ranking. SalonsFlow dispatches feedback prompts directly to customer messaging handles 60 minutes after POS checkout, driving positive reviews and organic client leads.

Summary Evaluation

While Vagaro and Fresha are ideal for Western markets, they introduce high billing commission friction and lack local conversational AI interfaces. ReSpark provides basic billing templates but lacks advanced webhook intelligence. SalonsFlow bridges these gaps by providing localized, commission-free booking automation built for Indian salon workflows.

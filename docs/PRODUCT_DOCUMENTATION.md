# Product Documentation: SalonFlow SaaS Platform

## 1. Vision & Mission
*   **Vision**: To empower every neighborhood salon and spa across the Indian sub-continent with enterprise-grade automation, enabling seamless booking, customer retention, and AI-driven growth.
*   **Mission**: To eliminate the friction of scheduling, maximize repeat customer rates, and streamline operations for salon owners using messaging-first technology (WhatsApp) and lightweight, high-performance dashboards.

---

## 2. Target Market
SalonFlow is optimized for the **Indian Salon & Beauty Market**, specifically targeting:
*   **Tier 1 & Tier 2 Cities**: Cities like New Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad, and Jaipur, where digital adoption and WhatsApp engagement are extremely high.
*   **Sub-Segments**:
    1.  *Independent Premium Salons*: Single-location premium outlets requiring appointment scheduling, staff allocation, and reviews tracking.
    2.  *Salon Chains (SMB)*: 2–5 outlet chains needing unified multi-tenant oversight and marketing campaign coordination.
    3.  *Neighborhood Parlors*: Highly localized shops requiring simple missed-call and voice note bookings to replace physical registers.

---

## 3. Customer Personas

### Persona A: Rajesh, The Salon Owner
*   **Profile**: 42 years old, runs "Rajesh Hair & Style" in South Delhi. Managing 8 staff members.
*   **Pain Points**: Customers frequently call while stylists are busy; lost bookings; forgets to ask for Google Reviews; struggles to run marketing promotions manually.
*   **SalonFlow Solution**: Automated missed-call welcomes, AI Hinglish chatbot for booking, review collection links, and the AI Rebooking Queue.

### Persona B: Priya, The Busy Customer
*   **Profile**: 29 years old, corporate employee working hybrid hours.
*   **Pain Points**: Doesn't want to download a separate salon booking app; hates calling to check open slots; prefers scheduling appointments via WhatsApp voice notes during commutes.
*   **SalonFlow Solution**: Dynamic voice note booking and chatbot confirmation entirely inside WhatsApp.

## 4. Pricing Model
SalonFlow operates on a value-based, three-tiered subscription model designed for scale in the Indian market:

| Plan | Price (INR / Month) | Target Audience | Features Included |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **FREE** | ₹0 | Neighborhood shops | Core manual booking dashboard, basic client directory, manual scheduling. |
| **BASIC** | ₹1,499 | Growing salons | Custom AI receptionist, missed call bookings, review collection automation. |
| **PRO** | ₹2,999 | Premium salons & chains | Voice note booking, bulk WhatsApp marketing campaigns, AI Rebooking Engine (Auto-Send). |

# Sales Playbook: SalonFlow Platform

This guide documents the sales pitches, interactive demos, objection handling, and onboarding workflows for bringing salons onto SalonFlow.

---

## 1. Onboarding Checklist for Sales Reps

1.  **Sign-Up**: Guide the owner to sign up via Clerk on the Dashboard. This triggers the signup webhook and seeds a default salon profile.
2.  **Service & Staff Seeding**: Log services (Haircut, Facial, Grooming) and staff names.
3.  **WhatsApp Integration**: Assist the owner in linking their WhatsApp Business phone number on Meta's developer portal.
4.  **Google Review Mapping**: Enter their salon's Google Business profile review URL and adjust the review delay slider to 60 minutes.
5.  **AI Receptionist Seed**: Customize the AI prompt based on their brand voice and price cards.
6.  **Dry-Run Booking Test**: Send a voice note and text booking to verify intent recognition and slot locks before going live.

---

## 2. Objection Handling Scripts

### Objection A: "I prefer using a paper register."
*   **Response**: *"I completely understand, paper feels simple. But what happens when you're washing a client's hair and the phone rings? You miss it. With SalonFlow, that missed call triggers an instant WhatsApp booking chat. You capture the customer without picking up the phone. In our pilots, this alone recovered 12 appointments per month."*

### Objection B: "Our customers speak Hindi and Hinglish. AI won't understand them."
*   **Response**: *"Actually, SalonFlow is built specifically for Indian salons. It understands voice notes in Hindi and text messages like 'Kal haircut ke liye slot khali hai kya?'. The AI responds in natural Hinglish script so your clients feel like they are chatting with a receptionist."*

### Objection C: "₹1,499 is too expensive."
*   **Response**: *"Let's look at the numbers. A single premium haircut and beard trim package is ₹600. Recovering just three missed calls or sending two rebooking reminders pays for the entire monthly subscription. Everything after that is direct profit."*

---

## 3. Interactive Demo Flow

1.  **The Missed Call Magic**: Have the salon owner call the demo number from their mobile and hang up. Show them the welcome message arriving on their WhatsApp within 5 seconds.
2.  **Voice Note Booking**: Have them send a voice note: *"Kal subah 11 baje haircut book kar do."* Show them the instant response: *"Booked! Haircut scheduled for tomorrow at 11:00 AM."*
3.  **Live Takeover Panel**: Open the dashboard on your tablet. Show them the live chat log updating in real-time, and toggle the AI switch off to demonstrate manual takeovers.
4.  **Rebooking Queue**: Show them the rebooking queue where the AI recommends follow-ups for clients who visited 30 days ago, illustrating the "Approve & Send" button.
5.  **Reviews Demo**: Complete an appointment. Send them the review request template. Have them click the link and show them the redirect to the Google Reviews page.

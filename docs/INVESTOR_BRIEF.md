# Investor Brief: SalonFlow

## 1. Market Opportunity (TAM / SAM / SOM)

*   **TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: **$10 Billion**. The total market size of the Indian Salon, Spa, and Wellness industry, which is expanding at a 12% CAGR.
*   **SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)**: **$1.2 Billion**. saloons and spas in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities in India that are actively digitizing and looking to move away from offline registers.
*   **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: **$150 Million**. Our target market size within 5 years, capturing 12% of digitized Indian salons through messaging-first and missed-call solutions.

---

## 2. Revenue Model & Monetization

SalonFlow generates revenue through two primary streams:
1.  **SaaS Subscriptions**: Recurring monthly subscriptions (Free, ₹1,499 Basic, ₹2,999 Pro) gating premium capabilities (bulk campaigns, voice note processing, AI rebooking).
2.  **WhatsApp Utility Markups**: A slight usage-based markup on WhatsApp Cloud API conversations (beyond Meta's free tier), matching customer notifications and bulk campaign broadcasts.

---

## 3. Unit Economics & LTV/CAC Ratio

*   **Average Revenue Per Account (ARPU)**: ₹2,100 ($25) per month.
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: ₹6,000 ($72). Obtained through digital marketing and hyper-local salon onboarding networks.
*   **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: ₹75,600 ($900). Calculated based on an average customer lifespan of 36 months.
*   **LTV/CAC Ratio**: **12.5x**. Highly profitable unit economics due to low onboarding friction and viral growth mechanisms (customers see "Booked via SalonFlow" inside WhatsApp).

---

## 4. Growth & Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

*   **The "Missed Call" Lead Magnet**: Offering salons a free missed-call webhook. When clients call, they receive an automated WhatsApp receptionist booking link. This low-friction onboarding acts as a strong top-of-funnel acquisition channel.
*   **Google Reviews Leverage**: Demonstrating immediate ROI by getting salons 20-30 organic Google Reviews in their first month, which directly increases their footfall on Google Maps.
*   **Hyper-local Referral Loop**: Incentivizing local salon managers with subscription credits for referring neighboring beauty parlors.

---

## 5. Pilot Salon Performance Metrics

Key outcomes compiled across our initial NCR (National Capital Region) pilot cohort:
*   **Bookings Automated**: 85% of standard appointment bookings handled end-to-end by the AI Receptionist without human intervention.
*   **Repeat Visit Lift**: A **22% increase** in repeat customer visits within 60 days of activating the AI Rebooking Engine.
*   **Google Rating Increase**: Average Google Maps ratings improved from `4.2` to `4.8` for pilot salons using the Automated Reviews module.

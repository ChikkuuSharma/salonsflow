## [2026-06-10T00:26:00+05:30] — Promotion of Out-of-Scope Features to Epic 7 (Stripe Payments & Load Balancing Scheduler)

### Decision
Promote Stripe checkout link generation in chat and multi-staff load-balancing scheduling from MVP Non-Goals to target functional requirements under a newly defined Epic 7. Additionally, introduce daily conversation limiters and Whisper voice note processing caps as cost and platform abuse safeguards.

### Rationale
Driven by Vikram (COO) operations and cost research. Enabling direct Stripe booking links in WhatsApp allows transactional commission collection, and staff load-balancing mitigates double-booking stylist limits. Conversation session caps and voice length constraints prevent runaway Meta/OpenAI expenses.

### Changes
*   Added **Epic 7: Advanced Booking Operations & In-Chat Monetization** with Requirement 7.1 (Stripe Checkout Links) and Requirement 7.2 (Multi-Staff Load Balancing).
*   Added WhatsApp Daily Session caps and OpenAI Whisper Audio length caps to Section 3: Non-Functional & Security Criteria.
